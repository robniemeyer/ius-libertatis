Ius Libertatis

Filius Humanitatis <filiushumanitatis@protonmail.com>

Based on Natural Law

I. Foreword

The legitimacy of the law lies in its simplicity, ensuring that even children can understand it.

The legitimacy of the law lies in its universality, ensuring that all human beings live free and with equal rights.

The legitimacy of the law lies in its timelessness, ensuring that the meaning of its content never changes.

II. Foundation

Love is based on consensually giving another person what you wish he/she to give you.

Justice is based on taking proportionately from another person what he/she has taken or threatens to take from you.

III. The Law

  1. I. It is forbidden to violate another person's body;
  2. II. It is forbidden to violate another person's property;
  3. III. It is forbidden to break contracts;
  4. IV. Justice is permitted against all those who violate these laws.

IV. Clarification

Self-defense does not constitute a violation of the law.

Reproduction of works does not constitute a violation of the law.

A contract is a public declaration of verifiable authorship.

Justice must be based on law and applicable to all individuals, without any distinction between human beings.

Judges must respect the freedom to object to the prosecution.

V. Law Enforcement

The death penalty may be claimed by the injured party or by another person on his/her behalf in cases of premeditated violation of the person's body, respecting the proportionality of the threat or damage.

Compensation may be claimed by the injured party or by another person on his/her behalf in cases of violation of the person's body or property, respecting the proportionality of the threat or damage.

Breach of contract may only be claimed by the injured party and in accordance with the terms of the contract.

VI. Setback

A witness who makes a false accusation must be sentenced to the same penalty claimed, with additional compensation to the victim.

A judge who hands down an unjust conviction must be sentenced to the same penalty he/she imposed, with additional compensation to the victim.

A convicted person who fails to comply with a court order within the established period may be subjected to detention and forced labor to serve the sentence, with additional compensation to the victim and the judge.

VII. Acknowledgement

I thank my family.

I thank the libertarians of all times.
